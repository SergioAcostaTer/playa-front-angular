Welcome to Playa Dorada

Playa Dorada is one of the most beloved beaches on the Canary Islands, offering a perfect blend of relaxation, adventure, and natural beauty. Known for its golden sands and crystal-clear waters, this beach has something for everyone. Whether you're looking to unwind with a cocktail by the sea or experience thrilling water sports, Playa Dorada is the ideal destination for your next beach getaway.

Beach Features

The beach stretches for 2 kilometers, offering plenty of space to enjoy the sun, sand, and sea. With its shallow waters, it’s ideal for families with children and beginner swimmers. For those seeking more adventure, there are plenty of activities available, from surfing to jet skiing. The calm waters make it a fantastic location for paddleboarding and snorkeling as well.

Amenities

Playa Dorada is equipped with a range of top-tier amenities to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable experience for all visitors:

  • Sunbeds & Umbrellas: Available for rent to provide a perfect place to relax under the sun.
  • Beachside Cafes & Restaurants: Offering a variety of delicious meals, including fresh seafood and tropical drinks. You can enjoy a refreshing cocktail while taking in stunning ocean views.
  • Restrooms & Showers: Clean facilities are available, including well-maintained showers and restrooms for your convenience.
  • Lifeguard Services: Our certified lifeguards are on duty from 9 AM to 6 PM to ensure the safety of all beachgoers.
  • Free Wi-Fi: Stay connected while relaxing on the beach with free Wi-Fi access available throughout the area.

Activities

If you're looking for more than just sunbathing, Playa Dorada offers a variety of activities to keep you entertained:

  • Surfing: Whether you're a beginner or an experienced surfer, Playa Dorada’s waves offer something for everyone. Surf lessons and board rentals are available.
  • Paddleboarding: Glide over the clear waters with paddleboarding, which is perfect for those who enjoy a relaxing but active water sport.
  • Beach Volleyball: Our volleyball courts are free to use for guests, offering a fun activity to enjoy with friends and family.
  • Jet Ski Rentals: For the more adventurous, you can rent a jet ski and explore the coastline from the water, or take part in a thrilling guided jet-ski tour.
  • Snorkeling & Scuba Diving: Discover the vibrant marine life beneath the waves with snorkeling or diving excursions, available with local guides.
  • Sunset Yoga: Join us for evening yoga sessions on the beach, perfect for unwinding while watching the sunset over the ocean.

Dog-Friendly & Accessibility

We pride ourselves on being an inclusive and welcoming beach for all visitors:

  • Dog-Friendly: Playa Dorada is a dog-friendly beach, so you can bring your furry friends to enjoy the sun and surf with you. Just make sure to keep them on a leash and clean up after them!
  • Wheelchair Accessible: The beach is fully accessible, with pathways leading from the parking area to the beach. We also offer wheelchair-friendly sunbeds for those who need them.

Upcoming Events

Playa Dorada hosts a variety of exciting events throughout the year:

  • Beach Parties: Every Friday night, enjoy live music, dancing, and bonfires with fellow beach lovers.
  • Annual Surf Competition: Playa Dorada hosts a popular surfing competition every summer, attracting talented surfers from around the world.
  • Seasonal Festivals: Throughout the year, the beach celebrates different local festivals, offering live performances, food stalls, and entertainment for the whole family.

Weather & Best Time to Visit

The weather at Playa Dorada is perfect year-round, with temperatures ranging from 22°C (72°F) in the winter to 28°C (82°F) in the summer. The summer months are perfect for sunbathing and water activities, while the winter offers a more peaceful, quieter experience. Ideal times to visit are from May to October when the weather is warm and most of the beach’s events are taking place.

Pricing

We offer affordable pricing for all our services to ensure that Playa Dorada is accessible for everyone:

  • Sunbeds & Umbrellas: Starting at $50 per day (includes two sunbeds and one umbrella).
  • Surf Lessons: Group lessons for $40 per person, private lessons for $70 per hour.
  • Jet Ski Rentals: Starting at $80 for a 30-minute ride, guided tours available at $120 for 45 minutes.
  • Yoga Classes: $15 per session for beach yoga at sunset.

Availability

Playa Dorada is open daily from 8 AM to 8 PM. Bookings for sunbeds and activities are highly recommended, especially on weekends and holidays, as we tend to fill up quickly.
